Hair Transplant

Hair Transplantation is a surgical technique that removes hair follicles from one part of the body, and is transplanted to a bald or balding part of the body. This treatment can be offered to male & females. The most popular treatment areas for a hair transplant are the head and the beard area. Pain is minimal as the procedure is carried out under local anesthetic, but you may experience some mild discomfort at the transplant site as well as in the area where hair was taken from for a few days following the procedure. Side effects are minimal, the most common is scarring as this cannot be avoided with any procedure.
