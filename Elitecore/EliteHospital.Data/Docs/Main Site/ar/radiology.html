Dr. ASMA MIHDAWI
GYNECOLOGIST AND OBSTETRICIAN

SpecialityGYNECOLOGIST AND OBSTETRICIAN

Dr. Asmaa has a bachelor’s degree for medicine and surgery from the University of Science and Technology, obtained in 1992. In 1993 she started the residency program in obstetrics and Gynaecology in the Islamic Hospital in Amman, after which she became a specialist in obstetrics and Gynaecology in 1998. In 1999 she worked as an OBGYN in the Islamic Hospital for 5 years, where she ran her own clinic and performed many vaginal and CS deliveries, as well as different types of gynecological and obstetrics operations. In 2005, Dr. Asmaa moved to Qatar where she worked at Qatar medical center for 14 years as a consultant in OBS and GYN. She dealt with different cases of obstetrics and Gynaecology; antenatal, postnatal, family planning, infertility and infections. In 2019 Dr Asma joined the obstetrics and Gynaecology team at Elite Medical Center putting more than 20 years of experience treating different cases in obstetrics and Gynaecology.
